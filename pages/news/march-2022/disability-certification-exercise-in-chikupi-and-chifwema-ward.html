Disability Certification exercise in Chikupi and Chifwema (Kafue District)

16 March 2022

On 16th March 2022, Twatasha together with Cheshire CBID Programme, Kafue General Hospital and Zambia Agency for Persons with Disabilities (ZAPD) conducted Disability Certification at Chifwema and Chikupi Wards within Kafue District.

We would like to thank Cheshire CBID Programme , the Zambia Agency for Persons with Disabilities (ZAPD) Provincial Office, Kafue General Hospital and other stakeholders for the support offered during the Disability Certification exercise conducted at Chikupi and Chipapa within Kafue District.

A considerable number of persons with disabilities managed to be certified by the doctors from Kafue General Hospital. Their details were immediately captured by ZAPD.

We would like to also appreciate Chikupi Primary School and Chipapa Local Clinic for providing us a suitable venue for conducting the disability certification.

We shall continue promoting inclusiveness by supporting persons with disabilities within our district.
