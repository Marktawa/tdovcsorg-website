Twatasha Disabled and OVC Organisation partners with NHIMA

5 January 2022

Twatasha Disabled and OVC Organisation in partnership with the National Health Insurance Management Authority (NHIMA), do hereby inform all persons with disabilities and the aged (65 years and above), are eligible to be registered as beneficiaries of National Health Insurance.

This registration will promote access to quality health care among the beneficiaries. The registration is free and all registered clients during this exercise will be exempted from monthly subscription in line with the National Health Insurance Act.

Those that missed this rare chance to take part in this registration exercise with NHIMA which took place on Tuesday, January 4th 2022 should not fret because another registration exercise will take place on Thursday, January 6th 2022 at Twatasha Community School, Kafue, from 0800 to 1400. Those that want to be registered should bring their NRC and disability cards so that they can be registered. Come one come all.
